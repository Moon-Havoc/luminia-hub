ACCESS_RULES

Rule set

Normal

24-hour lifetime. Reuse while active, regenerate after expiry.

Premium

Never expires. Issued and revoked by staff through Discord.

DM Delivery

Sensitive keys are sent privately instead of being exposed in public channels.

Username Match

Validation succeeds only when the Roblox username matches the one recorded with the key.

Moderation, premium access, blacklist control, HWID reset, and diagnostics all live inside Discord.
